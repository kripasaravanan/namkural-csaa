# NamKural — நம் குரல் (Our Voice) 🇺🇸

**NamKural** delivers free cybersecurity education, AI-powered risk assessments, and digital literacy programs across the United States at no cost. Our mission is to protect communities in the digital age by making digital safety and technological resources accessible to everyone.

## 🚀 Core Programs & Initiatives

### 🧠 1. AI-Powered Education
We leverage artificial intelligence to provide dynamic, accessible, and personalized digital safety training. This initiative focuses on teaching communities how to identify digital threats, secure personal data, and navigate the internet safely.

### 🛡️ 2. Cyber Risk Assessments
Empowering individuals and communities to understand their digital vulnerability. Our platform evaluates digital hygiene and provides clear, actionable risk scores to prevent cyber incidents before they happen.

### 📝 3. Research & Whitepapers
We actively research emerging digital threats and publish comprehensive whitepapers. These documents guide policymakers, educators, and community leaders in building stronger, more secure digital ecosystems.

### 🤝 4. Volunteer Network & Workshops
Digital literacy starts at the grassroots level. Our nationwide volunteer network drives in-person and virtual community workshops to ensure marginalized and at-risk populations are not left behind in the digital revolution.
